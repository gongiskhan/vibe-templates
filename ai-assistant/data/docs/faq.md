# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is this AI Assistant?
This is an AI-powered assistant designed to help you with various tasks, answer questions, and process documents. It uses advanced language models to provide helpful, accurate responses.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, your data is handled securely. Conversations are stored in session files and are not shared with third parties. Document uploads are processed locally and deleted after analysis.

### What languages are supported?
The assistant primarily supports English, but can understand and respond in multiple languages.

## Features

### Can I upload documents?
Yes! You can upload PDFs, Word documents, images, and text files. The assistant will analyze them and extract relevant information.

### What types of questions can I ask?
You can ask almost anything! The assistant is designed to help with:
- General knowledge questions
- Document analysis and summarization
- Task guidance and workflows
- Information lookup from the knowledge base

### How does the knowledge base work?
The knowledge base contains documents that the assistant can search through to provide accurate answers. When you ask a question, relevant documents are retrieved and used to inform the response.

## Technical Questions

### Do I need to create an account?
No account is required. Sessions are managed automatically using unique session IDs.

### How long are sessions stored?
Sessions are stored as JSON files on the server. The retention period depends on your deployment configuration.

### Can I integrate this with other systems?
Yes, the assistant provides API endpoints that can be integrated with other systems. See the developer documentation for details.

## Troubleshooting

### The assistant isn't responding
Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, check your network connection.

### My document won't upload
Ensure your document is in a supported format (PDF, DOC, DOCX, images, or text files) and under the size limit.

### The responses seem off
Try rephrasing your question with more specific details. The assistant works best with clear, detailed requests.
